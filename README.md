## 🚀 Overview

**Fetch** is a dog-themed entertainment app featuring sound effects, whistle sounds, and playful prank screens. With a simple and colorful design, the app provides a fun experience for dog lovers.

## 📥 Download & Install

Experience Fetch on your Android device immediately.

**[➔ Download Fetch APK (Latest)](https://github.com/ajy-ocean/fetch/releases/latest/download/fetch.apk)**

> **Note:** Since this is a direct APK install, you may need to "Allow installation from unknown sources" in your Android settings.
